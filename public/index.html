What We Do

End-to-End Hardware Lifecycle Services

From pickup to final disposition, we manage the complete journey of your retired IT equipment with transparency and care.

💾

Hardware Recycling

R2 / e-Stewards aligned recycling for laptops, desktops, servers, networking gear, mobile devices, and accessories. Materials are recovered and reintroduced into the supply chain.

🔒

Secure Data Destruction

NIST 800-88 compliant data sanitization, including software wiping, degaussing, and physical shredding. Each device receives a Certificate of Destruction.

🔧

Refurbishment & Resale

Functional equipment is tested, cleaned, and refurbished to working condition, then resold through trusted secondary-market channels — returning value back to you.

📥

IT Asset Disposition

Comprehensive ITAD programs for enterprises: on-site pickup, asset tagging, serialized inventory, financial settlement reporting, and audit-ready documentation.

How It Works

A Simple, Transparent Process

Four straightforward steps from initial contact to final reporting.

1

Inventory & Quote

Submit a list of equipment or describe what you'd like to retire. We respond within 24 hours with a transparent quote and pickup plan.

2

Secure Pickup

We coordinate logistics nationwide. Equipment is tracked from the moment it leaves your facility under documented chain-of-custody.

3

Process & Sanitize

Devices are sorted, tested, and either refurbished or dismantled for recycling. All storage media undergoes certified data destruction.

4

Report & Settle

You receive serialized inventory reports, Certificates of Destruction, environmental impact summaries, and any applicable value-recovery payment.
